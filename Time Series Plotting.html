# Summary
Both Calls Received and Urgent Calls Received have a seasonality to them. 
They seem to be in an uptrend with seasonality attributes. 
In times where there are more overall calls, there will probably be more urgent calls as well.
This could be a city that is growing in size, and so they are experiencing more calls over time. 
With this comes a higher number of urgent calls as well.
Calls seem to pick up during the late summer months.

Both of the predictive models ended up having an elevated prediction for the next 6 months due to using a mean average of prior months. 
Upon charting both columns we realize that using a mean average forecast is not the most accurate because it is taking the average of the surge months,
with the slower times of the year. Instead of predicting that the next 6 months values to drop due to seasonality, it predicts values that are closer
in range and more related to a average.
